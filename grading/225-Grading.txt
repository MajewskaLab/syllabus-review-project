Students with a 'B' average (80%) or better over all 5 tests may exempt out of the Final Exam and substitute their 5 test average for their Final Exam grade. See file “Exempting the Final Exam” on ELC site for details of procedure and requirements for exempting final exam 
All assignments are due by 8:00am on the due date. Late Assignments will not receive credit unless due to illness. If due to illness you must provide an authorized doctor's excuse.
No make-up Tests or Final Exam allowed without an authorized doctor's excuse.
