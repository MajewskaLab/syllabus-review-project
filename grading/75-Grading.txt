The grading will be managed electronically on eLC ("Quizzes & Exams" folder/module). Exams are 100 points each. You can decide whether or not to attend the final exam. If you take the final exam, your lowest score from the five exams will be dropped. There will be NO make-up exams. Neither can any exam be rearranged to another time. If you must miss a unit exam, you must take the final exam and the score will replace your missing unit exam. 

You may take the quiz twice and your highest grade will used for your final grade for that quiz. Quizzes are 30 points each. Like exams, there will be NO make-up quizzes.
