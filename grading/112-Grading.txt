Final grades for the course will be based on the final class averages (total score/5). The instructors will use a modified “distribution gap method” to determine the final grade brackets. In the past, the instructors have often used the class average as a C+/B- transition. However, the final placement of the grade brackets will be decided based on careful consideration of a multiple factors including the pattern of attendance and the level of participation of students whose scores are in proximity to the gaps in the score distribution.
