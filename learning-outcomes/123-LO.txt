Learning Objectives:
By the end of this course, students should be able to:
1. Identify and describe the major groups of marine organisms.
2. Compare and contrast the features of different marine habitats, and assess the ecological and evolutionary adaptations of the organisms present in each habitat.
3. Summarize how marine organisms interact with each other and their environment.
4. Interpret and explain scientific figures and technical research articles.
5. Predict the future impacts of human activities on marine organisms and ecosystems
