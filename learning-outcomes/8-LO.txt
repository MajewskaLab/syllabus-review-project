At the end of this course, you will expand your abilities to:

Apply principles of cellular and molecular biology to real world situations like evaluating how healthful a certain food is or whether you should be vaccinated for a certain disease

Identify and evaluate valid sources of scientific information.

Analyze and apply scientific information to make everyday decisions.

Explain how scientists test scientific claims and compose scientific arguments.

Communicate scientific ideas and build scientific arguments orally and in writing.
