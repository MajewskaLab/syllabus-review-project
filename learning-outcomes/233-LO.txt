Upon completion of this course, you should be able to: 
• understand the basic neurophysiology that underlies sensory coding across all sense modalities.
• know the basic anatomy and physiology of each sense modality.
• understand the role the brain plays in interpreting sensory information to give rise to perception.
• appreciate the importance of both physiological and behavioral studies to our understanding of human sensation and perception.
• explain the area of psychophysics and how it relates the physical world to perception
