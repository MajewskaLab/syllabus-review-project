Learning Objectives:
 
1.	Able to read original literature and understand the biological question you address in your project
2.	Able to design experiments to investigate gene functions, and understand relevant experimental methods 
3.	Able to write a research proposal on your project 
4.	Able to perform basic bioinformatics analysis for molecular cloning and phylogenetic analysis of genes
5.	Able to perform basic molecular cloning techniques 
6.	Able to generate transgenic Arabidopsis plants for functional studies of genes 
7.	Able to identify gene knock-out mutants in Arabidopsis
8.	Able to record and analyze experimental results and solve experimental problems
9.	Able to prepare and present your research findings in a poster.
