Upon successful completion of this course, you will be able to:
1) Describe the essential components and mechanisms of DNA replication, chromatin
modifications, transcription, and translation.
2) Describe eukaryotic chromosomes at different stages of the cell cycle, mitosis, and meiosis.
3) Infer information about genes, alleles, and gene functions from analysis of genetic crosses
and patterns of inheritance.
4) Describe multiple levels of regulation of gene expression.
5) Predict the phenotypic consequences of different kinds of mutations and chromosome
abnormalities.
6) Interpret the results of experiments using modern tools of molecular genetics and genomics
