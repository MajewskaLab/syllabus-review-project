Students will be exposed to the basic and applied study of microorganisms in their environment. 
At the successful completion of this course students will be able to:
• Describe the nature of environmental habitats for different groups of microbes
• Define the role of the microbial community in biogeochemical cycles and how this affects and is affected by human activities
• Recognize the role of the environment in infectious diseases
• Be familiar with the wide range of tools and methodologies used to study microbes in the environment and their activities
