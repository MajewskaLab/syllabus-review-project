Upon successful completion of the course, students should understand:  
•	Basic neurophysiology with an emphasis on synaptic transmission
•	Principles of pharmacology
•	Function and neuroanatomy of the major neurotransmitter systems
•	Effects of major psychoactive drugs on consciousness and behavior through their specific actions on neurotransmitter systems
•	Original research reports from the psychopharmacological literature and be able to critically evaluate them.
