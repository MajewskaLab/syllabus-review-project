Upon completion of this course, the student will: 
1) Understand the basic principles and practical applications of fish physiology 
2) Understand the influence of environmental conditions on physiology 
3) Discuss and evaluate primary literature in fish physiology 
4) Be introduced to the effects of contaminants on fish physiology 
5) Be able to evaluate quality of various sources of information 
6) Develop skills in critical thinking, writing, and oral expression
