By the end of this course, 

- You should have a better understanding of system structure and function at the levels of organisms, populations, communities, and ecosystems.
- You should be able to think in both a reductionist and holistic capacity, and utilize these perspectives to examine novel ecological situations. 
- The ecological background presented in this course will provide you with the tools to evaluate environmental problems confronting society, and
understand how to conserve and/or manage systems for future generations. 
- This course is also designed to prepare you for more advanced courses in ecology, such as population and community ecology, behavioral ecology, limnology, and systems ecology.
