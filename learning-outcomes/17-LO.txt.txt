Learning Goals

Be able to think critically about scientific information, and the relevance of scientific information to your career path.

Develop scientific literacy and the ability to acquire and assess new knowledge and present this to others.

Build confidence in your ability to understand and make decisions about biology in your daily life.

Make connections among different topics in biology and connections between human behavior and the many biological challenges we face.

Understand core biological concepts including ecology, evolution, biodiversity, transformations of energy and matter, human body systems, and interactions between humans and their environment.
